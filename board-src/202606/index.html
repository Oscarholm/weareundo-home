Country performance

The Netherlands (+15% YoY, ahead of plan) and Finland (+44% YoY, well ahead on margin) carried the quarter. Sweden grew +29% YoY but margins slipped against budget. Norway revenue was broadly in line, with margins below plan following staff interruptions from mid-February; a substantial revenue reduction of approximately 50% is expected in June and July, with 2 of 3.5 FTEs on short-notice sick leave. Denmark recorded its first revenue (pre-revenue in Q1 2025).

Group EBITDA variance

Corporate costs increased €167K YoY to €255K (+191%), reflecting planned central investment: WAU staff costs more than doubled as the central team was built out, alongside the brand programme and NetSuite licence and implementation — spend lines that did not exist in the prior year. Against budget, Corporate is only €12K over (+4.9%). Underlying clinic operations improved year-on-year.

Full commercial and clinic-level KPI reporting follows the Beam and NetSuite implementations per market, contingent on completion of the merger.
